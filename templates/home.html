ROOMS

Standard Room

The standard room at our hotel offers a comfortable and affordable accommodation option for travelers looking for a cozy place to stay.

Amenities
  • A double or queen-sized bed with soft linens and comfortable pillows.
  • A TV with access to a variety of cable and satellite channels for entertainment
  • A small desk with a chair and lamp, which serves as a workspace.
  • A private en-suite bathroom equipped with a shower, sink, and toilet. Basic amenities such as towels, soap, shampoo, and toilet paper are also provided.
  • Guests can enjoy free Wi-Fi internet access throughout the room and hotel

    • Rs 1500 per night

Deluxe room

Our deluxe room offers a luxurious and spacious accommodation option for travelers who want to indulge in comfort and elegance during their stay

Amenities
  • A king-sized bed or two double beds with premium linens and plush pillows
  • A seating area with a sofa or armchairs with TV
  • A large desk with a chair and lamp, which serves as a workspace
  • A private en-suite bathroom equipped with a shower, sink, toilet, luxurious toiletries, fluffy towels, and bathrobes
  • Guests can enjoy free Wi-Fi internet access throughout the room and hotel
  • additional perks such as a mini-fridge, coffee maker, and complimentary breakfast

    • Rs 2500 per night

Suite

Our suite is designed to make your stay with us a truly unforgettable experience. As you step into our suite, you'll immediately be struck by the tasteful decor and attention to detail. With a spacious living area, a separate bedroom, and a private balcony with stunning views

Amenities
  • Bedroom features a comfortable king-size bed with plush bedding
  • A flat-screen TV and an en-suite bathroom with premium toiletries, fluffy towels, and a luxurious bathtub
  • Suite's living area has a large flat-screen TV, and a minibar stocked with a selection of beverages and snacks
  • A private balcony fitted with a lounge chair and a round table to enjoy your morning coffee
  • Exclusive access to our hotel's amenities, including our state-of-the-art fitness center, luxurious spa, and swimming pool

Rs 5000 per night
